In TOGAF, the **Architecture Landscape** provides a **high-level view** of all the architectures within an organization.
It shows **what architecture exists today**, **what is being developed**, and **what is planned for the future**.

Think of it as a **map of the entire enterprise architecture** across different time horizons.

# **ğŸ¯ Purpose of the Architecture Landscape**

The Architecture Landscape helps you:

* Plan the **future (target) architecture**
* Identify gaps, overlaps, or inconsistencies
* Provide visibility to stakeholders
