## **What Happens in Phase G (Implementation Governance)**

In Phase G:

* **Projects and delivery teams implement the solution**
* **Architecture team provides governance and oversight**

So the implementation **does happen**, but **NOT by the architecture team**.

---

| Activity                       | Who Does It?                     |

| Approval / waiver decisions    | Architecture Board               |
| Architecture guidance          | Enterprise / Solution Architects |

## **What Phase G DOES**

* Reviews implementations against approved architecture
* Issues **Architecture Contracts**
* Approves or rejects deviations
* Provides architecture guidance
* Ensures alignment with Architecture Vision and Roadmap
