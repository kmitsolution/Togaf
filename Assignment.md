## **Assignment 1: Architecture Vision (Phase A)**

### **Scenario**

A mid-size retail company wants to improve customer experience by launching **omnichannel digital services** (web + mobile).

### **Tasks**

1. Identify **business drivers** and **business goals**.
2. Define **architecture principles** (minimum 5).
3. Identify key **stakeholders** and their concerns.
4. Create a **high-level Architecture Vision** (1 page).
5. List **success criteria** for this initiative.

### **Deliverables**

* Architecture Vision document
* Stakeholder map
* Principles list

## **Assignment 2: Business Architecture (Phase B)**

### **Scenario**

The retail company struggles with slow order fulfillment and inconsistent customer data.

### **Tasks**

1. Create a **Business Capability Map** (baseline & target).
2. Identify **business process gaps**.
3. Perform **Business Gap Analysis**.
4. Propose improvements aligned with the Architecture Vision.

### **Deliverables**

* Capability map
* Gap analysis table
* Improvement recommendations

## **Assignment 3: Information Systems Architecture (Phase C)**
