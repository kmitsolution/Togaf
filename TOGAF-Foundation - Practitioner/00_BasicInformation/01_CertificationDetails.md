### **TOGAF¬Æ Architecture Foundation Certification ‚Äì Introduction**

The **TOGAF¬Æ Architecture Foundation certification**, part of The Open Group's certification program, validates that a candidate has acquired knowledge of the **basic concepts**, **core principles**, and **terminology** of **Enterprise Architecture** based on the **TOGAF¬Æ Standard, 10th Edition**.

This entry-level certification confirms that the candidate understands:

* The **TOGAF¬Æ standard's structure and key components**
* The **Architecture Development Method (ADM)** and its phases
* Core **Enterprise Architecture principles**
* Basic **TOGAF¬Æ definitions**, terminology, and concepts
* The purpose of **content frameworks**, **building blocks**, and **architecture views and viewpoints**
* The roles and responsibilities in the TOGAF framework
