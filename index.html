TOGAF® Part 1 – MCQ Quiz

  

1. TOGAF covers four architecture domains. Which one completes the list?

✅ Correct Answer: Business
TOGAF defines four domains: Business, Data, Application, and Technology.

2. Where is practical guidance on how to apply TOGAF provided?

✅ Correct Answer: TOGAF Series Guides
Series Guides provide practical, how-to guidance for applying TOGAF.

3. What are the levels of the Architecture Landscape?

✅ Correct Answer: Foundation, Common, Solution
These represent reusable levels across the enterprise.
